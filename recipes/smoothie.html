PB & J Protein Smoothie

Description

My go-to protein shake. Naturally sweet and filling and manages to make the protein powder palatable.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • A big fat spoonful of peanut butter
  • 1 scoop of unflavored protein powder
  • 10 ounces of you liquid of choice (I'm using oatmilk)

Steps

  1. Bust out the blender.
  2. Throw all the ingredients in the blender.
  3. Blend for at least a minute until it's smooth.
  4. Enjoy!
